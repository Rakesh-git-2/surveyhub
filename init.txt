## **📅 Phase 1: MVP (Minimal Viable Product) – 3 Months**  
### **1️⃣ Core Features & Pages**  
✅ **User Authentication**  
- Login / Signup (Google, Email, Social Logins)  
- Forgot Password / Reset  

✅ **Dashboard Page**  
- Overview of active surveys  
- Quick stats (Total Responses, Engagement Rate)  
- Recent surveys & shortcuts to reports  

✅ **Survey Creation Page**  
- Drag-and-drop survey builder  
- Question types: MCQ, Likert Scale, Open-ended, Yes/No, Rating  
- Conditional Logic (skip/branch based on answers)  
- Auto-save & Draft mode  

✅ **Survey Distribution Page**  
- Generate shareable links  
- Embed code for websites  
- QR code & social media sharing  
- Email & SMS integrations  

✅ **Survey Response Page (For Participants)**  
- Mobile-friendly UI  
- Progress tracker  
- Option to save & continue later  

✅ **Basic Survey Report Page**  
- View response count & demographics  
- Export data (CSV, Excel)  
- Simple bar charts & pie charts  

✅ **User Management Page**  
- Edit profile (name, email, avatar)  
- Manage subscription (Free vs. Paid Plan)  
- Invite team members (for Business Plan)  

✅ **Admin Panel (For Internal Use)**  
- User management  
- Survey moderation  
- Payment tracking  

---

## **📅 Phase 2: Advanced Reporting & AI (Months 4-6)**  
### **2️⃣ AI-Enhanced Features & Pages**  
✅ **Advanced Reporting Page**  
- AI-powered summary of responses  
- Sentiment analysis on open-ended answers  
- Identify key trends (e.g., recurring complaints)  
- Word cloud for most common words  

✅ **AI-Generated Recommendations Page**  
- Actionable insights based on survey data  
- Compare results to industry benchmarks  
- Predictive analytics for trend forecasting  

✅ **Automated Report Generation**  
- Generate professional PDF reports  
- Customizable sections (summary, charts, recommendations)  
- Schedule & auto-send reports to stakeholders  

✅ **Survey Engagement Metrics Page**  
- Track how long users spend answering  
- Identify high drop-off questions  
- A/B testing to optimize survey effectiveness  

✅ **Survey API & Integrations Page**  
- Connect with Google Sheets, Slack, Zapier  
- Webhooks for real-time response tracking  

✅ **Notification System**  
- Email alerts for new responses  
- Automated reminders for incomplete surveys  

---

## **📅 Phase 3: Scaling & AI Enhancements (Months 7-12)**  
### **3️⃣ AI & Personalization Features**  
✅ **Conversational AI Surveys (Chatbot-Like Experience)**  
- AI-guided question flow  
- Smart follow-up questions based on previous answers  

✅ **AI-Powered Survey Auto-Generation**  
- Auto-create surveys based on industry type  
- Smart suggestions for questions & answer choices  

✅ **Multi-Language Support**  
- AI-powered translation for global users  
- Detects participant language & adjusts automatically  

✅ **User Role Management & Permissions**  
- Different access levels (Admin, Editor, Viewer)  
- Audit logs for security  

✅ **Mobile App Development (iOS & Android)**  
- Push notifications for new responses  
- Offline survey collection (sync later)  

✅ **Performance & Scalability Enhancements**  
- Load balancing for high-traffic surveys  
- Optimized database for faster queries  

---

# 🎯 **Tech Stack for Development**  
💻 **Frontend:** React.js + Tailwind CSS + Next.js (for SSR)  
⚙️ **Backend:** Node.js + Express.js  
📦 **Database:** PostgreSQL (structured survey data) + Redis (caching)  
🤖 **AI Services:** OpenAI GPT, TensorFlow, VADER (Sentiment Analysis)  
📡 **Hosting:** AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda), Firebase for Authentication  

---

# 🏁 **Final Deliverables & Timeline**  
✅ **MVP Launch (Month 3)** – Basic survey creation, response collection, and reporting  
✅ **AI-Powered Reporting (Month 6)** – Smart insights, sentiment analysis, recommendations  
✅ **Full Feature Rollout (Month 12)** – Conversational AI, mobile apps, real-time analytics
